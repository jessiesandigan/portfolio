Jessie Sandigan
works profile contact

(b. 1991) is a visual artist who engages in various art forms, including drawing, painting, and mixed media. Taking out inspiration from a range of sources, particularly biblical references, Sandigan examines the relationship between humans and the divine through his visual language.
